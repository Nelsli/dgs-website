About Us

Development and Growth Strategies LLC is a two-person grant writing team focused on supporting non-profit organizations.  While we specialize in community health, we've assisted a variety of organizations as they develop programs and expand services. We've written state and federal grants, including The Bureau of Primary Health Care, the Federal Office of Rural Health Policy and the United States Department of Agriculture. We have helped secure over $25 million for our clients, many of whom operate Federally Qualified Health Centers in rural areas and are working to establish health networks and address health information technology needs.

“DGS understands the mission of our company, and they keep their eyes open for opportunities that will help us grow.” - Shannon Adams

Phyllis Platt, PhD, MSW

Phyllis has nearly twenty-five years of experience working with non-profits and community based organizations. She earned a Masters in Social Work from The Southern Baptist Theological Seminary in Louisville, KY and a PhD from the University of Kentucky. Phyllis has taught at the University of Kentucky and Spalding University, where she also served as Chair of the School of Social Work.

Phyllis has expertise in school based health, rural health, integrated care, non-profit leadership, and organizational capacity building. Her primary interest is in helping address community needs though partnerships and collaboration.

Lindsay Nelson, MSW

Lindsay brings her passion for social justice to her work by developing strategies and programs that are focused on improving disparities and increasing access to opportunities. Through this equity lens, Lindsay has advocated for policies that expand access to health insurance, developed an expansive outreach and enrollment program, and provided training to a statewide workforce.

Lindsay earned a Bachelors in Psychology from Gettysburg College and a Masters in Social Work from Spalding University.
