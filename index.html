About Us

Development and Growth Strategies LLC is a two-person grant writing team focused on supporting non-profit organizations. While we specialize in community health, we have assisted a variety of organizations as they develop programs and expand services. We've written state and federal grants (including BPHC, ORHP, and USDA) that have helped secure over $20 million for our clients, many of whom operate Federally Qualified Health Centers in rural areas.

Phyllis Platt

Phyllis has over 20 years of experience working with non-profits and community organizations. SW from SBTS and PhD from University of Kentucky

Lindsay Nelson

Lindsay is passionate about social justice and improving help disparites. She has a Masters in Social Work from Spalding University.
