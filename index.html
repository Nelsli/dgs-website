About Us

Development and Growth Strategies LLC is a two-person grant writing team focused on supporting non-profit organizations.  While we specialize in community health, we've assisted a variety of organizations as they develop programs and expand services. We've written state and federal grants, including The Bureau of Primary Health Care, the Federal Office of Rural Health Policy and the United States Department of Agriculture. We have helped secure over $20 million for our clients, many of whom operate Federally Qualified Health Centers in rural areas and are working to establish health networks and address health information technology needs.

“The ongoing support DGS Grants offers allows me to be 100% confident in all of my grant applications and reports”

Phyllis Platt, PhD, MSW

Phyllis has over twenty years of experience working with non-profits and community organizations. Dr Platt has her Masters in Social Work from The Southern Baptist Theological Seminary and a PhD from the University of Kentucky. Phyllis taught at Spalding University and served as the Social Work Program Chair. Phyllis has expertise in school-based health, rural health, integrated care, and organizational capacity building.

Lindsay Nelson, MSW

Lindsay is passionate about social justice and improving health disparities. She has experience with outreach and enrollment programs, behavioral health, conducting needs assessments, and providing training. Lindsay has her Masters in Social Work from Spalding University.
